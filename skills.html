Teamwork and Sportsmanship...

Game On

Play your first game of the season.
Work hard, have fun!

High Five

High five a teammate after a great shift or when they need encouragement.

High Five

Be a good sport. High five your opponents after a great game.

Puck Pursuit...

The Collector

Skate hard and chase down a loose puck. Once you have it, hold on!

The Thief

Skate quickly and steal the puck back whenever your opponent gets it.

The Protector

You earned the puck! Protect it! Don't pass it to the other team or dump it to open ice.

Skating...

Crossovers

Turn by placing one foot over the other.

Hockey Stop

Turn your hips and feat quickly and dig in to the ice.

Pivot

Skate forward and spin to begin skating backward. You'll be moving faster from the start.

Stickwork...

Stick Check

Pull up on your opponents stick to steal the puck.

Poke Check

Tap the puck away from your opponent with your stick.

Kick to Stick

Puck in your feet?
Kick it to your stick.

Scoring...

Light the Lamp

Score a goal!
Sounds easy, right?

The Helper

Pass the puck to a teammate who then scores.

Plus Player

Be on the ice when your team scores.
